Explain how a CPU processes instructions step by step
From Module 4 — Computer Architecture
The CPU processes instructions through the Fetch-Decode-Execute cycle. First it fetches the instruction from RAM using the Program Counter. Then the Control Unit decodes it into micro-operations. Finally the ALU executes it and stores the result. Your lab notes on page 34 include a diagram of this cycle for your upcoming assessment.
